# Instagram Analyzer

A privacy-focused Instagram Relationship Analyzer that helps you discover:

* Followers
* Following
* Non-followers (people you follow who don't follow you back)

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your Instagram data never leaves your device.

## Live Website

Visit the website here:

**https://aswins404.github.io/Instagram_Analyzer/**

---

## Features

* View all Instagram followers
* View all Instagram following accounts
* Identify non-followers instantly
* Search usernames
* Export non-followers to a TXT file
* Detailed relationship information
* See when someone followed you
* See when you followed them
* Determine who followed first
* Calculate follow-back delay between two users
* Mobile-friendly interface with swipe navigation
* Works completely in the browser
* No Instagram login required
* No server required
* Supports large Instagram exports
* Supports multiple follower files (`followers_1.json`, `followers_2.json`, etc.)

---

## Privacy

This project is designed with privacy in mind.

* No data is uploaded to any server
* No analytics or tracking
* No Instagram credentials required
* All ZIP processing happens locally in your browser

Your Instagram export remains on your device at all times.

## How to Use

### 1. Download Your Instagram Data

1. Open Instagram
2. Go to **Accounts Center**
3. Select **Your Information and Permissions**
4. Choose **Download Your Information**
5. Request and download your Instagram export

### 2. Open the Analyzer

Visit the website and upload the downloaded ZIP file.

### 3. Analyze Your Data

The analyzer will automatically:

* Load followers
* Load following
* Compare both lists
* Display non-followers
* Show follow timestamps
* Show who followed first
* Show follow-back delay information

### 4. View Relationship Details

Click any username to see:

* Relationship status
* Follow timestamps
* Who followed first
* Follow-back delay
* Direct profile link (when available)

### 5. Export Results

Click **Export Non-Followers TXT** to save the results.
